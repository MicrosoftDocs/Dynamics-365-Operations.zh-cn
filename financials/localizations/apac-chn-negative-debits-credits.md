<span data-ttu-id="5b254-104">本主题说明如何在中国的总帐中显示两个单独列中的会计科目的借方和贷方金额。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="5b254-104">This topic shows how to display credit and debit amounts for ledger accounts in two separate columns in the general ledger for China.</span></span>
