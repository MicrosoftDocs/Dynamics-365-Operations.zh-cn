# <a name="master-planning-home-page"></a><span data-ttu-id="3d0d8-103">主计划主页</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3d0d8-103">Master planning home page</span></span>

[!include [banner](../includes/banner.md)]

<span data-ttu-id="3d0d8-104">在其核心，主计划允许公司确定和平衡原材料和产能的将来需求，以实现公司目标。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3d0d8-104">At its core, Master planning allows companies to determine and balance the future need for raw materials and capacity to meet company goals.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="3d0d8-105">主计划评估以下情况：</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3d0d8-105">Master planning assesses the following:</span></span> 

-  <span data-ttu-id="3d0d8-106">哪些原材料和产能当前可用？</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3d0d8-106">What raw materials and capacities are currently available?</span></span> 
-  <span data-ttu-id="3d0d8-107">完成生产需要哪些原材料和产能？</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3d0d8-107">What raw materials and capacities are required to complete production?</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="3d0d8-108">例如，必须进行哪些生产、采购、转移或留出安全库存才能完成生产。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3d0d8-108">For example, what must be manufactured, purchased, transferred, or set aside as safety stock before you can complete production.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="3d0d8-109">主计划使用此信息来计算需求和生成计划订单。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3d0d8-109">Master planning uses the information to calculate the requirements and generate planned orders.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="3d0d8-110">三个主计划流程是：</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3d0d8-110">The three main planning processes are:</span></span>

-  <span data-ttu-id="3d0d8-111">**主计划** - 主计划计算净需求。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3d0d8-111">**Master planning** - The Master plan calculates net requirements.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="3d0d8-112">它基于当前的实际订单，使公司在短期内每日控制库存补货。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3d0d8-112">It is based on actual current orders and enables companies to control inventory replenishment on a short-term, day-to-day basis.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="3d0d8-113">描述它的另一个术语是*净需求计划*。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3d0d8-113">Another term to describe it is the *Net requirements plan*.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="3d0d8-114">有关详细信息，请参阅[主计划](master-plans.md)。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3d0d8-114">For more information, see [Master planning](master-plans.md).</span></span> 

-  <span data-ttu-id="3d0d8-115">**预测计划** - 预测计划计算总需求。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3d0d8-115">**Forecast planning** - The Forecast schedule calculates gross requirements.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="3d0d8-116">它基于将来的推测（或预测），让公司可以执行长期的物料和产能规划。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3d0d8-116">It is based on future projections (or forecasts), and enables companies to conduct long-term planning of materials and capacity.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="3d0d8-117">有关详细信息，请参阅[预测计划](introduction-demand-forecasting.md)。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3d0d8-117">For more information, see [Forecast planning](introduction-demand-forecasting.md).</span></span> 

-  <span data-ttu-id="3d0d8-118">**内部公司主计划** - 内部公司主计划跨法人计算净需求。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3d0d8-118">**Intercompany master planning** - The Intercompany master plan calculates net requirements across legal entities.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="3d0d8-119">它连接公司之间的需求和供应，不仅是短期的固定需求和供应，还有长期的计划（还未确定）需求和供应。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3d0d8-119">It connects demand and supply between companies not only for short term, firm demand and supply but also for long-term, planned (that is not yet firmed) demand and supply.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="3d0d8-120">有关详细信息，请参阅[内部公司主计划](https://mbspartner.microsoft.com/AX/CourseOverview/1276)（网络培训）（需要 CustomerSource 帐户）。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3d0d8-120">For more information, see [Intercompany master planning](https://mbspartner.microsoft.com/AX/CourseOverview/1276)  (eLearning) (requires CustomerSource account).</span></span> 

<span data-ttu-id="3d0d8-121">公司可以更改计划的输出。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3d0d8-121">Companies can change the output of the plan.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="3d0d8-122">他们可以运行再生改变、净改变，或二者。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3d0d8-122">They can run regenerative, net change, or both.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="3d0d8-123">再生计划更新所有需求，而净改变计划只更新在上次计划编制运行后有新需求的项目计划。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3d0d8-123">Regenerative plans update all requirements, whereas, net change plans only update the plan on items with new requirements that have come in since the last scheduling run.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="3d0d8-124">主计划编制计划通常涉及短期，可以是从一个星期到六个月的任何时间段。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3d0d8-124">Master scheduling plans typically involve the short term, which can be anywhere from one week to six months.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="3d0d8-125">主计划模块确定将满足当前需求（净需求）的供应（材料）和产能（资源）。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3d0d8-125">The Master planning module determines the supply (materials) and capacity (resources) needs that will meet current demand (the net requirements).</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="3d0d8-126">在大多数公司中，这扩展为在要接收的产品中包含最久的累积提前期。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3d0d8-126">In most companies this is extended to include the longest cumulative lead time among the products to be received.</span></span>
