# <a name="target-entities"></a><span data-ttu-id="17832-103">目标实体</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="17832-103">Target entities</span></span>

[!include [banner](../../includes/banner.md)]

 

<span data-ttu-id="17832-104">在 **数据管理** 工作区中，可以获取目标实体、关联实体类型和与 **资产管理** 模块关联的暂存表的概览。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="17832-104">In the **Data management** workspace, you can get an overview of target entities, related entity types and staging tables related to the **Asset management** module.</span></span>

2. <span data-ttu-id="17832-106">在 **导入/导出** 部分中，单击 **数据实体**。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="17832-106">In the **Import / Export** section, click **Data entities**.</span></span> 

    ![图 1](media/01-data-management.png)

3. <span data-ttu-id="17832-108">在 **目标实体** 页面上，在筛选器中搜索“资产管理”，然后按 Enter 以查看与资产管理关联的实体的列表。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="17832-108">On the **Target entities** page, search for "asset management" in the filter, and press Enter to see a list of the entities related to Asset management.</span></span>

    <span data-ttu-id="17832-109">下图显示部分资产管理实体。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="17832-109">The figure below shows some of the Asset management entities.</span></span>

   ![图 2](media/02-data-management.png)
