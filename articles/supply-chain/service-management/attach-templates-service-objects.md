# <a name="attach-templates-to-service-objects"></a><span data-ttu-id="474da-103">将模板附加到服务对象</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="474da-103">Attach templates to service objects</span></span>    

[!include [banner](../includes/banner.md)]


<span data-ttu-id="474da-104">物料清单 (BOM) 模板可以附加到某一服务对象关系。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="474da-104">A template bill of materials (BOM) can be attached to a service object relation.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="474da-105">然后，可以将该服务对象关系与服务订单或服务协议相关联。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="474da-105">The service object relation can then be associated with either service orders or service agreements.</span></span>

1.  <span data-ttu-id="474da-106">单击**服务管理** \> **设置** \> **服务对象** \> **服务对象**。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="474da-106">Click **Service management** \> **Setup** \> **Service objects** \> **Service objects**.</span></span>

2.  <span data-ttu-id="474da-107">选择某一服务对象。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="474da-107">Select a service object.</span></span>

3.  <span data-ttu-id="474da-108">单击**服务协议**或**服务订单**，以便显示该服务对象相关联的服务协议或服务订单。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="474da-108">Click **Service agreements** or **Service orders** to display the service agreements or service orders that the service object has been associated with.</span></span>

4.  <span data-ttu-id="474da-109">选择服务协议或服务订单，单击**功能**，然后单击**附加物料清单模板**。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="474da-109">Select a service agreement or a service order, click **Functions**, and then click **Attach template BOM**.</span></span>

5.  <span data-ttu-id="474da-110">选择要附加到该对象的物料清单模板。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="474da-110">Select the template BOM to attach to the object.</span></span>


> [!TIP]
> <P><span data-ttu-id="474da-111">若要将某一附加的物料清单移到其他服务对象或删除附加的物料清单，请在<STRONG>服务对象</STRONG>窗体中，单击<STRONG>功能</STRONG>，然后单击<STRONG>移动物料清单模板</STRONG>或<STRONG>删除物料清单模板</STRONG>。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="474da-111">To move an attached BOM to another service object or to delete an attached BOM, in the <STRONG>Service objects</STRONG> form, click <STRONG>Functions</STRONG>, and then click <STRONG>Move service BOM</STRONG> or <STRONG>Delete service BOM</STRONG>.</span></span></P>
