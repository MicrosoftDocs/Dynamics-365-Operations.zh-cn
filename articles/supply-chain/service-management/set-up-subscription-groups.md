[!include [banner](../includes/banner.md)]


1.  <span data-ttu-id="5cc33-104">单击**服务管理** \> **设置** \> **服务预订** \> **预订组**。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="5cc33-104">Click **Service management** \> **Setup** \> **Service subscriptions** \> **Subscription groups**.</span></span>
