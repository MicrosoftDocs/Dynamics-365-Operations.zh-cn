<span data-ttu-id="75d63-104">此主题描述如何查看服务订单中未从派遣团队分配工作人员的活动。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="75d63-104">This topic describes how to view activities in a service order that don’t have a worker assigned to them from a dispatch team.</span></span>

    > [!NOTE]
