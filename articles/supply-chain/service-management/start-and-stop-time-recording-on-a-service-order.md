## <a name="start-time-recording"></a><span data-ttu-id="15b5f-105">开始时间记录</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="15b5f-105">Start time recording</span></span>

## <a name="stop-time-recording"></a><span data-ttu-id="15b5f-109">停止时间记录</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="15b5f-109">Stop time recording</span></span>
