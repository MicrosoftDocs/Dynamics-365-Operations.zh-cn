## <a name="create-a-service-agreement-from-service-management"></a><span data-ttu-id="094dc-105">从服务管理创建服务协议</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="094dc-105">Create a service agreement from Service management</span></span>

> [!NOTE]
> <span data-ttu-id="094dc-114">如果您将来自其他服务协议的行复制到该服务协议中，则可以指示是否还要复制服务对象和服务任务关系。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="094dc-114">If you copy lines into the service agreement from another service agreement, you can indicate whether you also want to copy service object and service task relations.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="094dc-115">如果您复制这些关系，则它们将添加到该服务协议的任何现有关系上。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="094dc-115">If you copy these relations, they are added to any existing relations on the service agreement.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="094dc-116">如果您从某一服务模板复制服务协议行，则服务对象和服务任务关系将自动复制为新的服务协议行上的服务对象关系和服务任务关系。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="094dc-116">If you copy service-agreement lines from a service template, the service-object and service-task relations are automatically copied as service-object relations and service-task relations on the new service-agreement lines.</span></span>
