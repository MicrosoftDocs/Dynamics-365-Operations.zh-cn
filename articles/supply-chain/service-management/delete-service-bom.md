[!include [banner](../includes/banner.md)]


1.  <span data-ttu-id="aa0e2-104">单击 **服务管理** \> **常用** \> **服务协议** \> **服务协议**。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="aa0e2-104">Click **Service management** \> **Common** \> **Service agreements** \> **Service agreements**.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="aa0e2-105">选择您要从中删除服务物料清单的服务协议。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="aa0e2-105">Select the service agreement that you want to delete a service bill of materials (BOM) from.</span></span>
