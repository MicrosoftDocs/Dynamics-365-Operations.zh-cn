# <a name="reassign-activities"></a><span data-ttu-id="60b07-103">重新分配活动</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="60b07-103">Reassign activities</span></span> 

[!include [banner](../includes/banner.md)]


<span data-ttu-id="60b07-104">本主题描述如何将服务活动从一个工作人员重新分配给另一个工作人员。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="60b07-104">This topic describes how to reassign service activities from one worker to another worker.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="60b07-105">您可以轻松地将一个工作人员的服务活动重新分配给其他工作人员，即使这些工作人员被分配到不同的派遣团队。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="60b07-105">You can reassign service activities from one worker to another worker even if those workers are assigned to different dispatch teams.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="60b07-106">使用以下步骤将活动重新分配给另一个工作人员：</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="60b07-106">Use the following steps to reassign an activity to another worker:</span></span>

1.  <span data-ttu-id="60b07-107">单击 **服务管理** \> **定期** \> **派遣板**。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="60b07-107">Click **Service management** \> **Periodic** \> **Dispatch board**.</span></span>

2.  <span data-ttu-id="60b07-108">在 **开始日期** 和 **结束日期** 字段中,输入日期定义时间段以查看活动。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="60b07-108">In the **From date** and **To date** fields, enter dates to define the time period to view activities for.</span></span>

3.  <span data-ttu-id="60b07-109">选择是否查看已关闭的活动和相关团队的派遣信息，然后单击 **确定**。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="60b07-109">Select whether to view closed activities and dispatch information for related teams, and then click **OK**.</span></span>

4.  <span data-ttu-id="60b07-110">在 **派遣板** 窗体中,单击 **高级** 以在页面的顶部显示甘特图。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="60b07-110">In the **Dispatch board** form, click **Advanced** to display only the Gantt chart at the top of the page.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="60b07-111">单击 **简单** 以在该页的顶部显示甘特图和在窗体中显示选项卡。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="60b07-111">Click **Simple** to display the Gantt chart and the tabs in the form.</span></span>

5.  <span data-ttu-id="60b07-112">展开各派遣团队列表。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="60b07-112">Expand each dispatch team list.</span></span>

6.  <span data-ttu-id="60b07-113">使用以下步骤之一重新分配活动：</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="60b07-113">Use either of the following steps to reassign an activity:</span></span>
    
      - <span data-ttu-id="60b07-114">选择表示服务活动的颜色编码的图形以重新分配。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="60b07-114">Select a color-coded graphic that represents the service activity to reassign.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="60b07-115">按下 SHIFT 键，然后将该彩色编码的图形移到其他员工的行。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="60b07-115">Press the SHIFT key, and then move the color-coded graphic to the row for another employee.</span></span>
    
      - <span data-ttu-id="60b07-116">在 **所有**、**团队**、**未发货** 或 **相关** 选项卡上的 **工作人员** 字段中，输入替换当前工作人员活动的工作人员的姓名。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="60b07-116">On the **All** tab, **Team** tab, **Undispatched** tab, or **Related** tab, in the **Worker** field, enter the name of the worker who is replacing the current worker for the activity.</span></span>
