[!include [banner](../includes/banner.md)]

<span data-ttu-id="95ba2-104">服务协议组用于对服务协议进行排序和筛选。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="95ba2-104">Service agreement groups are useful for sorting and filtering service agreements.</span></span>
