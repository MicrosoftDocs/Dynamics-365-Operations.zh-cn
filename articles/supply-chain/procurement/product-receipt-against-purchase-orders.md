<span data-ttu-id="2efbb-105">产品收货是录制已收到所订购的产品的流程，以便可以处理采购订单 (PO) 以进行开票。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2efbb-105">Product receipt is the process of recording that products that were ordered have been received, so that the purchase order (PO) lines can then be processed for invoicing.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="2efbb-106">在某些情况下，产品经过预登记，其中在收到产品前记录来自供应商的其他信息。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2efbb-106">In some cases, products go through preregistration, where additional information from the supplier is recorded before the products are received.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="2efbb-107">当产品到达时，它们第一次被标记为**已登记**。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2efbb-107">When products arrive, they are first marked as **Registered**.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="2efbb-108">在产品最后被标记为**已接收**前，它们可能经过其他流程，如质量管理。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2efbb-108">The products might then go through additional processes, such as quality management, before they are finally marked as **Received**.</span></span>

## <a name="preregistration-asn"></a><span data-ttu-id="2efbb-109">预登记 (ASN)</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2efbb-109">Preregistration (ASN)</span></span>

## <a name="registration"></a><span data-ttu-id="2efbb-117">注册</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2efbb-117">Registration</span></span>
<span data-ttu-id="2efbb-118">产品收货登记通常发生在仓库的进货台。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2efbb-118">Product receipt registration often occurs at the inbound docks in a warehouse.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="2efbb-119">其通过使用手持设备或通过到达日记帐执行。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2efbb-119">It’s performed either by using a hand-held device or through arrival journals.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="2efbb-120">或者，可以通过使用**采购订单**页的**登记**操作手动登记产品收货。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2efbb-120">Alternatively, you can manually register product receipt by using the **Registration** action on the **Purchase order** page.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="2efbb-121">在这两种情况下，产品均被标记为**已登记**。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2efbb-121">In both cases, the products are marked as **Registered**.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="2efbb-122">请注意，产品还不会标记为**已接收**。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2efbb-122">Note that the products aren’t yet marked as **Received**.</span></span>  

<span data-ttu-id="2efbb-123">在仓库中接收的产品可能要经过质量检验，然后才放入库存。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2efbb-123">Products that are received in a warehouse might go through quality inspection before they are put away into inventory.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="2efbb-124">质检订单或检验单可以用来执行质量检查。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2efbb-124">Either quality orders or quarantine orders can be used to perform quality inspection.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="2efbb-125">如果使用质检订单，您可以将流程配置为在检查产品时通过保留暂时阻止产品。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2efbb-125">If quality orders are used, you can configure the process to temporarily block products through a reservation while they are inspected.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="2efbb-126">如果使用了检验单，产品移动到另一个仓库进行检查。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2efbb-126">If quarantine orders are used, products are moved to another warehouse for inspection.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="2efbb-127">此仓库被称为检验仓库。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2efbb-127">This warehouse is known as the quarantine warehouse.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="2efbb-128">在这两个质量检查流程中，某些货物可能报废，因为它们不符合质量期望，或因为质量检查涉及产品样本的破坏性测试。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2efbb-128">In both quality inspection processes, some of the goods might be scrapped, either because they don’t conform to the quality expectations or because the quality inspection involves destructive testing of a sample of the product.</span></span>
