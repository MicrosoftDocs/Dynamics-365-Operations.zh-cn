# <a name="landed-cost-module"></a><span data-ttu-id="eda66-103">登陆成本模块</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="eda66-103">Landed cost module</span></span>

[!include [banner](../../includes/banner.md)]

<span data-ttu-id="eda66-104">**登陆成本** 模块通过为用户提供从制造商到仓库的进口货运的完全财务和物流控制，帮助企业简化入站装运操作。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="eda66-104">The **Landed cost** module helps businesses streamline inbound shipping operations by giving users complete financial and logistical control over imported freight, from the manufacturer to the warehouse.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="eda66-105">对于进口商品，登陆成本可能占每个进口物料总成本的 40% 或更多。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="eda66-105">For imported goods, landed costs can account for 40 percent or more of the total cost of each imported item.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="eda66-106">因此，挑战在于提供准确的登陆成本估计。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="eda66-106">Therefore, the challenge is to provide accurate estimates for landed costs.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="eda66-107">企业可以使用登陆成本完成以下任务：</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="eda66-107">Businesses can use Landed cost to complete the following tasks:</span></span>

- <span data-ttu-id="eda66-108">在航行创建时估计登陆成本。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="eda66-108">Estimate landed costs at the time of voyage creation.</span></span>
- <span data-ttu-id="eda66-109">在一个航行中将登陆成本分摊到多个物料和采购订单或转移单。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="eda66-109">Apportion landed costs to multiple items and purchase orders or transfer orders in a single voyage.</span></span>
- <span data-ttu-id="eda66-110">通过确认登陆成本支持物理位置之间的货物转移。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="eda66-110">Support the transfer of goods between physical locations by recognizing landed costs.</span></span>
- <span data-ttu-id="eda66-111">确认在途货物的应计。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="eda66-111">Recognize accruals for goods in transit.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="eda66-112">登陆成本为开销登陆成本提供准确、及时的成本估计。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="eda66-112">Landed cost provides accurate and timely cost estimates for overhead landed costs.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="eda66-113">同时，还在扩展的供应链中提供更高的财务和物流可见性。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="eda66-113">At the same time, it provides increased financial and logistical visibility into the extended supply chain.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="eda66-114">它还帮助减少成本计算和成本计算错误的管理。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="eda66-114">It also helps reduce the administration of costing and costing errors.</span></span>

## <a name="highlights"></a><span data-ttu-id="eda66-115">亮点</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="eda66-115">Highlights</span></span>

### <a name="voyages"></a><span data-ttu-id="eda66-116">航行</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="eda66-116">Voyages</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="eda66-117">在登陆成本中，航行是指从出站位置开始，在指定时间段内经过一组特定目的地，最终到达指定入站仓库位置的不同移动。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="eda66-117">In Landed cost, a voyage is a distinct movement from an outbound location, through a specific set of destinations over a specified period, to a specified inbound warehouse location.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="eda66-118">可以将采购订单和订单行添加到航行的一个集装箱或多个集装箱，成本将正确分配回物料行。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="eda66-118">Purchase orders and order lines can be added to either a single container or multiple containers for a voyage, and the costs will be correctly allocated back to the item line.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="eda66-119">订单和订单行也可以在一个航行中跨法人添加。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="eda66-119">Orders and order lines can also be added across legal entities for a single voyage.</span></span>

### <a name="item-ownership"></a><span data-ttu-id="eda66-120">物料所有权</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="eda66-120">Item ownership</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="eda66-121">在 Microsoft Dynamics 365 Supply Chain Management 中，货物通常是在仓库目的地接收，然后开票。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="eda66-121">In Microsoft Dynamics 365 Supply Chain Management, goods are typically received at the warehouse destination and then invoiced.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="eda66-122">但是，根据大多数国际贸易协定，企业从货物离开出发港口之时起，在货物被实际接收前拥有货物的所有权。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="eda66-122">However, under most international trade agreements, a business takes ownership of goods from the time when they leave the original port, before they have been physically received.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="eda66-123">登陆成本让企业能够在货物被实际接收前为货物开票。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="eda66-123">Landed cost enables businesses to invoice goods before they have been physically received.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="eda66-124">此概念被称为在途货物订单。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="eda66-124">This concept is known as a goods in transit order.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="eda66-125">它会在原始采购订单开票后创建新的订单类型来管理货物收货。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="eda66-125">It creates a new type of order to manage the receipt of goods after the original purchase order has been invoiced.</span></span>

### <a name="costs"></a><span data-ttu-id="eda66-126">成本</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="eda66-126">Costs</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="eda66-127">当您在登陆成本中创建航行时，成本可以自动添加到航行中。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="eda66-127">When you create a voyage in Landed cost, costs can automatically be added to it.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="eda66-128">这些成本在登陆成本中设置，有不同成本选项和成本基准。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="eda66-128">These costs are set up in Landed cost, and various cost options and cost bases are available for them.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="eda66-129">可以为航行的不同级别设置每个成本，然后通过支持数量、体积、重量、金额和定义的容量除数的分摊规则将其分摊到物料级别。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="eda66-129">Each cost can be set up for different levels of a voyage and apportioned to the item level through apportionment rules that support quantity, volume, weight, amount, and defined volumetric divisors.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="eda66-130">这些估计成本为航行提供所有成本的准确估计。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="eda66-130">These estimated costs provide an accurate estimate of all costs for a voyage.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="eda66-131">然后，登陆成本对估计登陆成本运行初步过帐/应计，以确保在航行创建时提供准确的估计成本计算。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="eda66-131">Landed cost then runs a preliminary posting/accrual of the estimated landed costs to ensure that an accurate calculation of estimated costs is provided at the time of voyage creation.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="eda66-132">在为这些自动成本开票时，估计成本将根据成本发票冲销，并被实际成本代替。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="eda66-132">As these automatic costs are invoiced, the estimated costs are reversed and replaced by the actual costs, based on cost invoices.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="eda66-133">实际成本是使用为每个成本类型（例如，关税、运费和保险费）设置的清算帐户在成本开票时过帐的冲销估计成本。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="eda66-133">Actual costs are reverse estimated costs that are posted at the time of cost invoicing by using clearing accounts that are set up for each type of cost (for example, duty, freight, and insurance).</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="eda66-134">这些过帐遵循与特定物料关联的过帐行为。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="eda66-134">These postings follow the posting behavior that is associated with the specific item.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="eda66-135">它们会自动更新库存过帐，不论过帐类型是先进先出 (FIFO)、后进先出 (LIFO)、移动加权平均值还是移动平均。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="eda66-135">They automatically update inventory posting, regardless of whether the posting type is first in, first out (FIFO), last in, first out (LIFO), moving weighted average, or moving average.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="eda66-136">支持所有库存模型组过帐类型。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="eda66-136">All inventory model group posting types are supported.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="eda66-137">对于移动平均和标准成本过帐，可以使用采购价格差异帐户过帐估计成本与实际成本之间的差额。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="eda66-137">For moving average and standard cost posting, purchase price variance accounts are available to post the differences between estimated costs and actual costs.</span></span>

### <a name="item-and-order-tracking"></a><span data-ttu-id="eda66-138">物料和订单跟踪</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="eda66-138">Item and order tracking</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="eda66-139">当航行从起始的出站位置移到最终的目标仓库时，用户可以根据需要更新其行程的每个步骤或 *支线*。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="eda66-139">As a voyage moves from the originating outbound location to the final destination warehouse, users can update each step, or *leg*, of its journey as required.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="eda66-140">对于每个支线，将确定提前期和装运状态。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="eda66-140">For each leg, a lead time and a shipment status are identified.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="eda66-141">另外还将确定转移到行程下一个支线的确认交货日期。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="eda66-141">Confirmed delivery dates for movement to the next leg of the journey are also identified.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="eda66-142">这些交货日期共同提供货物到达入站仓库的估计交货日期。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="eda66-142">Together, these delivery dates provide an estimated delivery date of the goods to the inbound warehouse.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="eda66-143">用户可以更改这些交货日期。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="eda66-143">Users can change these delivery dates.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="eda66-144">在这种情况下，货物的估计交货日期随后将根据行程的提前期和支线自动更新。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="eda66-144">In this case, the estimated delivery date of the goods is then automatically updated, based on the lead times and legs of the journey.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="eda66-145">在货物收货之前，可以按集装箱按航行和船只查看在途货物。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="eda66-145">Visibility into goods in transit by voyage and vessel is available on a per-container basis before receipt of the goods.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="eda66-146">由于日期在每个采购订单行上更新，因此企业可以更好地控制物流和仓库计划。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="eda66-146">Because the dates are updated on each purchase order line, businesses have more control over logistics and warehouse planning.</span></span>
