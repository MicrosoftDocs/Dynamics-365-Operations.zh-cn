## <a name="estimated-versus-used"></a><span data-ttu-id="55864-133">估计与已使用</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="55864-133">Estimated versus Used</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="55864-134">在 Field Service 中，工作订单内的产品和服务的数量和金额都有 **估计** 值和 **已使用** 值。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="55864-134">In Field Service, products and services on work orders have both **Estimated** values and **Used** values for quantities and amounts.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="55864-135">但是在 Supply Chain Management 中，销售订单没有相同的 **估计** 和 **已使用** 值概念。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="55864-135">However, in Supply Chain Management, sales orders don't have the same concept of **Estimated** and **Used** values.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="55864-136">若要在 Supply Chain Management 中为对销售订单使用预期数量的产品分配提供支持，但保留应消耗和开票的已使用数量，则需要通过两组任务同步工作订单中的产品和服务。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="55864-136">To support product allocation that uses the expected quantity on the sales order in Supply Chain Management, but to keep the used quantity that should be consumed and invoiced, two sets of tasks synchronize the products and services on the work order.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="55864-137">一组任务针对 **估计** 值，另一组任务针对 **已使用** 值。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="55864-137">One set of tasks is for **Estimated** values, and the other set of tasks is for **Used** values.</span></span>
