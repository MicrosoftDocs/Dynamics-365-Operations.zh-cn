# <a name="leave-and-absence-management-overview"></a><span data-ttu-id="14889-105">休假和缺勤管理概览</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="14889-105">Leave and absence management overview</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="14889-106">**休假爱喝缺勤管理**模块提供灵活的框架，用于定义缺勤管理流程。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="14889-106">The **Leave and absence management** module offers a flexible framework for defining the absence management process.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="14889-107">可以创建休假和缺勤计划以确定员工累积假期或如何赠予休假。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="14889-107">Leave and absence plans can be created to determine how employees accrue or are granted time off.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="14889-108">员工在计划中登记后，可以提交休假申请供经理审批。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="14889-108">After employees are enrolled in a plan, they can submit time-off requests for approval by managers.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="14889-109">休假跟踪荣一线经理和人力资源 (HR) 经理都可以看到谁在休假，以及每位员工剩余的假期量。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="14889-109">Leave tracking lets both first-level managers and Human Resources (HR) managers see who is taking time off and how much time off each employee still has.</span></span>  

<span data-ttu-id="14889-110">休假和缺勤管理提供以下功能：</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="14889-110">Leave and absence management provides the following features:</span></span> 

- <span data-ttu-id="14889-111">**定义休假和缺勤类型。**</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="14889-111">**Define leave and absence types.**</span></span>

    <span data-ttu-id="14889-112">休假类型定义员工可报告的各种缺勤类型。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="14889-112">Leave types define the various types of absences that employees can report.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="14889-113">由于这些类型由用户定义，所以可以根据组织进行调整。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="14889-113">Because these types are user-defined, they can be tailored to your organization.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="14889-114">典型休假类型包括带薪休假 (PTO)、休假、短期残疾、陪审员义务（这种休假类型特定于美国）和丧亡等。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="14889-114">Some typical leave types include paid time off (PTO), leave, short-term disability, jury duty (this leave type is specific to the United States), and bereavement.</span></span> 

- <span data-ttu-id="14889-115">**定义根据业务分层的休假和缺勤计划。**</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="14889-115">**Define leave and absence plans that are tiered to fit your business.**</span></span>

    <span data-ttu-id="14889-116">可以将休假和缺勤计划定义为按照特定频率（如年、月或半月）累积。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="14889-116">Leave and absence plans can be defined so that accrual occurs at specific frequencies, such as annually, monthly, or semimonthly.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="14889-117">还可以将计划定义为赠予，这样就在特定日期累积一次。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="14889-117">Plans can also be defined as a grant, where a single accrual occurs on a specific date.</span></span> 

    <span data-ttu-id="14889-118">休假计划中通常包含层，其中一些员工组根据其在组织中的时间享受更多福利。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="14889-118">Leave plans often contain tiers, where some groups of employees receive additional benefits, based on the amount of time that they have been with the organization.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="14889-119">这些用户定义的层允许根据定义的日期条件自动登记更多福利时数。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="14889-119">These user-defined tiers enable automatic enrollment in additional benefit hours, based on the date criteria that are defined.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="14889-120">也可以配置休假计划以实施结转量或最小余额，以防员工使用的福利时数超过累积量。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="14889-120">Leave plans can also be configured to enforce a maximum carry-over amount or a minimum balance, to prevent employees from using more benefit hours than they have accrued.</span></span> 

    <span data-ttu-id="14889-121">典型休假计划中包含分层计划，这些分层计划为新员工赠予 80 小时的 PTO 福利，但是工作 60 个月后则赠予 120 小时的福利。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="14889-121">Typical leave plans include tiered plans that grant a benefit of 80 hours of PTO to new employees but a benefit of 120 hours after 60 months of service.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="14889-122">更多计划可能赠予浮动假期或基于职位的福利，如仅限管理层的福利时数。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="14889-122">Additional plans might grant floating holidays or position-based benefits, such as executive-only benefit hours.</span></span>

- <span data-ttu-id="14889-123">**单独或通过基于工作条件的批量分配为员工登记休假和缺勤计划。**</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="14889-123">**Enroll employees in leave and absence plans individually or through mass assignment that is based on job criteria.**</span></span>

    <span data-ttu-id="14889-124">可以使用若干基于工作的筛选器将一组员工分配给休假计划。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="14889-124">Several job-related filters can be used to assign a group of employees to a leave plan.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="14889-125">HR 经理可以查看员工以了解为其登记了哪些休假和缺勤计划。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="14889-125">HR managers can view an employee to see what leave and absence plans the employee is enrolled in.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="14889-126">HR 经理还可以查看所有计划和相关的员工登记情况。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="14889-126">Alternatively, HR managers can view all plans and the related employee enrollments.</span></span>

- <span data-ttu-id="14889-127">**暂停休假和缺勤计划。**</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="14889-127">**Suspend leave and absence plans.**</span></span>

    <span data-ttu-id="14889-128">可以暂停休假和缺勤计划，以使其进入静止状态并防止增加累积量。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="14889-128">Leave and absence plans can be suspended to make them inactive and prevent additional accruals.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="14889-129">如果员工的雇用结束，将暂停其累积。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="14889-129">Accruals are suspended for employees if their employment ends.</span></span>  

- <span data-ttu-id="14889-130">**调整权利和赠予。**</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="14889-130">**Adjust entitlements and grants.**</span></span>

    <span data-ttu-id="14889-131">员工可以通过使用工作人员特定的日期覆盖其默认计划供应来在更高计划层中登记。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="14889-131">An employee can be enrolled in a higher plan tier by using a worker-specific date to override the employee's default plan offering.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="14889-132">计划登记时，员工可以收到对其休假和缺勤余额的年度调整，HR 也可以随时进行年度调整。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="14889-132">Employees can receive a manual adjustment to their leave and absence balance at the time of plan enrollment, or HR can make a manual adjustment at any time.</span></span> 

- <span data-ttu-id="14889-133">**对休假请求应用工作流。**</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="14889-133">**Apply a workflow to time-off requests.**</span></span>

     <span data-ttu-id="14889-134">可以自定义工作流并应用于休假请求，以满足组织的要求。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="14889-134">A workflow can be customized and applied to time-off requests to meet the organization’s requirements.</span></span>  

- <span data-ttu-id="14889-135">**跟踪员工缺勤余额。**</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="14889-135">**Track employee absence balances.**</span></span>

    <span data-ttu-id="14889-136">员工、其经理和 HR 可以查看休假和缺勤余额。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="14889-136">Employees, their manager, and HR can view leave and absence balances.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="14889-137">HR 可使用交互式报告按类型跟踪计划登记情况和休假余额。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="14889-137">HR can use interactive reports to track plan enrollments and time-off balances by type.</span></span> 

- <span data-ttu-id="14889-138">**提交休假请求。**</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="14889-138">**Submit time-off requests.**</span></span>

    <span data-ttu-id="14889-139">员工可针对其可用时数提交休假请求。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="14889-139">Employees can submit time-off requests against their available hours.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="14889-140">请求可以是简单的一天请求，也可以是包含多个休假和缺勤类型的多天请求。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="14889-140">Requests can be simple single-day requests or multiple-day requests that include multiple leave and absence types.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="14889-141">如果不启用工作流，将自动批准请求。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="14889-141">If a workflow isn't enabled, the requests are automatically approved.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="14889-142">如果启用工作流，可以自动执行审批，也可以要求签核，具体取决于工作流配置。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="14889-142">If a workflow is enabled, the approval can be automatic, or it can require sign-off, depending on the workflow configuration.</span></span>
