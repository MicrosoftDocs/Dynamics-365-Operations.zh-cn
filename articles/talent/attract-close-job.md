## <a name="cancel-a-job"></a><span data-ttu-id="555b2-107">取消职位</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="555b2-107">Cancel a job</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="555b2-108">您可以在 Attract 中随时取消职位。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="555b2-108">You can cancel a job in Attract at any time.</span></span>

   ![在 Attract 中关闭职位](./media/attract-close-job.png)

## <a name="close-a-job-as-position-filled"></a><span data-ttu-id="555b2-114">通过“职位已填补”关闭职位</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="555b2-114">Close a job as Position filled</span></span>

   ![在 Attract 中关闭职位](./media/attract-close-job.png)
