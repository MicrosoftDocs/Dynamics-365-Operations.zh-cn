[!include [banner](includes/banner.md)]

<span data-ttu-id="08786-104">本主题解释了如何设置客户的 Recency、频率和货币 (RFM) 分析。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="08786-104">This topic explains how to set up a Recency, Frequency, and Monetary (RFM) analysis of your customers.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="08786-105">Recency、频率和货币 (RFM) 分析是一个您的组织用来评估由客户购买生成的数据的市场营销工具。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="08786-105">Recency, frequency, and monetary (RFM) analysis is a marketing tool that your organization can use to evaluate the data that is generated by customer purchases.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="08786-106">在设置了 RFM 分析后，客户在购买时会被分配计算过的 RFM 得分。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="08786-106">After you set up RFM analysis, customers are assigned a calculated RFM score as they make purchases.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="08786-107">RFM 得分可以是一个三位等级，也可以是一个总数，具体取决于您的组织如何配置了 RFM 分析。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="08786-107">The RFM score can be a three-digit rating or an aggregate number, depending on how your organization has configured RFM analysis.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="08786-108">如果您的组织使用三位数等级进行评分，等级的作用方式如下：</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="08786-108">Here's how the rating works if your organization uses a three-digit rating for the score:</span></span>

- <span data-ttu-id="08786-109">第一位是客户的 Recency 等级，即客户最近一次从您的组织进行购买距离现在有多久。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="08786-109">The first digit is the customer's recency rating, which is how recently the customer made a purchase from your organization.</span></span>

- <span data-ttu-id="08786-111">第三位数为客户的货币等级，即客户在从您的组织进行购买时花费的货币量。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="08786-111">The third digit is the customer's monetary rating, which is how much the customer spends when he makes purchases from your organization.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="08786-112">例如，您的组织设置 1 至 5 的等级，其中 5 是最高等级。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="08786-112">For example, your organization has set the ratings on a scale of 1 through 5, where 5 is the highest rating.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="08786-113">在此情况下，客户等级 535 可告知您有关客户的以下信息：</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="08786-113">In this case, a customer rating of 535 tells you the following information about the customer:</span></span>
