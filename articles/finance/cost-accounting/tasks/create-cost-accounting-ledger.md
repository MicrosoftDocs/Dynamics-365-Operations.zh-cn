[!include [banner](../../includes/banner.md)]

<span data-ttu-id="7a680-104">成本核算分类帐表示整个报告单位。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7a680-104">A cost accounting ledger represents the overall reporting unit.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="7a680-105">它由成本元素维度、统计维度、会计日历和币种定义。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7a680-105">It is defined by a cost element dimension, statistical dimension, fiscal calendar, and currency.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="7a680-106">它对法人概念不可知。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7a680-106">It is agnostic to the concept of legal entities.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="7a680-107">可将法人及其数据与多个成本核算分类帐关联。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7a680-107">A legal entity and its data can be associated with many cost accounting ledgers.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="7a680-108">此录制使用 USP2 演示数据公司。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7a680-108">This recording uses the USP2 demo data company.</span></span>

1. <span data-ttu-id="7a680-109">转到“成本核算”>“分类帐设置”>“成本核算分类帐”。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7a680-109">Go to Cost accounting > Ledger setup > Cost accounting ledgers.</span></span>

5. <span data-ttu-id="7a680-113">在“会计日历”字段中，输入或选择一个值。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7a680-113">In the Fiscal calendar field, enter or select a value.</span></span>

8. <span data-ttu-id="7a680-116">在“统计维度”字段中，输入或选择一个值。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7a680-116">In the Statistical dimension field, enter or select a value.</span></span>
9. <span data-ttu-id="7a680-117">单击“保存”。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7a680-117">Click Save.</span></span>
