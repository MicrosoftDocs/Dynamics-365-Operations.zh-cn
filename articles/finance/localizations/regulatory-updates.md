<span data-ttu-id="1aa4f-109">Microsoft 致力于尽早实施新的监管要求。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="1aa4f-109">Microsoft strives to implement new regulatory requirements as early as possible.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="1aa4f-110">实际交付日期取决于法律公布日期、当地主管机构的监管详细信息公布情况、验证工具的推出情况，以及更改的幅度和复杂性。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="1aa4f-110">The actual delivery date depends on the law announcement date, availability of the requirement details from the local authorities, the availability of the validation tools, and on the size and complexity of the change.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="1aa4f-111">我们计划通过一个版本服务更新及时交付监管更新，以便客户进行更新和为（事务性监管更新的）实施日期做好准备，或为（与报告有关的监管更新）的第一个强制报告截止日期做好准备。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="1aa4f-111">We plan to deliver regulatory updates in One Version service updates that are released in time for customers to update and be ready for the enforcement date (for transactional regulatory updates), or for the first mandatory reporting deadline (for regulatory updates related to reporting).</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="1aa4f-112">客户和合作伙伴可以在预览版提前采用计划 (PEAP) 中预览新的监管更新。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="1aa4f-112">Customers and partners can preview the new regulatory updates in the Preview Early Adoption Program (PEAP).</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="1aa4f-113">如果公布日期晚、需求详细信息或验证工具推出时间晚，或更改极其大且复杂，可能无法在月更新正式发布日期推出监管更新。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="1aa4f-113">In case of late announcement dates, late availability of requirement details or validation tools, or exceptionally large and complex changes, it might not be possible to make a regulatory update available by the General Availability date of a monthly update.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="1aa4f-114">在这些情况下，监管更新将以相关可用月更新的修补程序的形式发布。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="1aa4f-114">In these cases, the regulatory update will be shipped as hot fixes for relevant available monthly updates.</span></span>
