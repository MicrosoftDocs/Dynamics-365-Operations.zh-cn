# <a name="display-debit-and-credit-amounts-in-ledger-accounts-for-china"></a><span data-ttu-id="a896e-103">显示中国会计科目的借方和贷方金额</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a896e-103">Display debit and credit amounts in ledger accounts for China</span></span>

[!include [banner](../includes/banner.md)]

<span data-ttu-id="a896e-104">本主题说明如何在中国的总帐中显示两个单独列中的会计科目的借方和贷方金额。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a896e-104">This topic shows how to display credit and debit amounts for ledger accounts in two separate columns in the general ledger for China.</span></span> 

<span data-ttu-id="a896e-105">您可以查看 **凭证交易记录** 查询页面中的两个单独列中的会计科目的贷方和借方金额。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a896e-105">You can view credit and debit amounts for ledger accounts in two separate columns on the **Voucher transactions** inquiry page.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="a896e-106">还可以查看与冲销条目的负金额。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a896e-106">You can also view the negative amounts that are related to the reversing entries.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="a896e-107">所有负金额在 **凭证交易记录** 查询页面中以红色字母显示。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a896e-107">All negative amounts are displayed in red letters on the **Voucher transactions** inquiry page.</span></span>

1.  <span data-ttu-id="a896e-108">单击 **总帐** &gt; **分类帐设置** &gt; **总帐参数**。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a896e-108">Click **General ledger** &gt; **Ledger Setup** &gt; **General ledger parameters**.</span></span>
2.  <span data-ttu-id="a896e-109">将 **在借方/贷方列中显示金额** 滑块设置为 **是**。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a896e-109">Set the **Show amount in debit/credit columns** slider to **Yes**.</span></span>
3.  <span data-ttu-id="a896e-110">打开 **总帐** &gt; **查询和报表** &gt; **凭证交易记录** 进行确认。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a896e-110">Open **General ledger** &gt; **Inquiries and reports** &gt; **Voucher transactions** to confirm.</span></span>
