<span data-ttu-id="f2502-106">快照使组织可以编辑和保存有关某个时间点其现金头寸和现金预测的信息。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f2502-106">Snapshots let organizations edit and save information about their cash position and cash forecasts at a point in time.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="f2502-107">您可以将快照与实际财务状况进行比较，检查差异，并使用该信息改进随时间变化的现金流预测。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f2502-107">You can compare the snapshot with actual financials, examine the variance, and use that information to improve cash flow forecasts over time.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="f2502-108">更特别的是，可通过以下方式使用快照：</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f2502-108">More specifically, snapshots can be used in the following ways:</span></span>

- <span data-ttu-id="f2502-109">跟踪现金头寸和现金预测。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f2502-109">Track cash position and cash forecasts over time.</span></span>
- <span data-ttu-id="f2502-110">筛选数据以显示为其创建快照的法人的子集。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f2502-110">Filter the data to show a subset of legal entities that the snapshot was created for.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="f2502-114">可以通过选择 **现金流预测** 工作区上 **现金头寸** 选项卡或 **现金预测** 选项卡上的 **新建快照** 创建快照。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f2502-114">You can create a snapshot by select **New snapshot** on either the **Cash position** tab or the **Cash forecast** tab on the **Cash flow forecasts** workspace.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="f2502-115">创建快照后，可以编辑和保存快照中的流入和流出。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f2502-115">After a snapshot is created, the inflows and outflows in it can be edited and saved.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="f2502-116">**快照** 选项卡中包含所有保存的快照。要打开快照，请选择其名称。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f2502-116">All saved snapshots are available on the **Snapshots** tab. <span data-ttu-id="f2502-117">随时可以编辑快照中的现金流入和流出。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f2502-117">The cash inflows and outflows in snapshots can be edited at any time.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="f2502-118">编辑流入金额或流出金额时，更新后的金额将按比例分配给创建原始余额的流动性科目中。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f2502-118">When an inflow amount or an outflow amount is edited, the updated amount is prorated to the liquidity accounts that made the original balance.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="f2502-119">快照编辑完毕后，请选择 **保存** 以保存更改。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f2502-119">When you've finished editing a snapshot, select **Save** to save your changes.</span></span>
