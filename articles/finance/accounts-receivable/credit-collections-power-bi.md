<span data-ttu-id="36d35-112">此 Power BI 内容包括 10 个报表页：</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="36d35-112">This Power BI content consists of 10 report pages:</span></span>

## <a name="setup-needed-to-view-power-bi-content"></a><span data-ttu-id="36d35-119">查看 Power BI 内容所需设置</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="36d35-119">Setup needed to view Power BI content</span></span>
