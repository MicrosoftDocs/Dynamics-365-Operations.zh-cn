<span data-ttu-id="bd10e-105">个性化在让用户和组织优化 Microsoft Dynamics 365 for Finance and Operations 中的用户体验以满足其需求中扮演着重要的角色。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="bd10e-105">Personalization plays an important role in allowing users and organizations to optimize the user experience in Microsoft Dynamics 365 for Finance and Operations to meet their needs.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="bd10e-106">有关个性化的更多详细信息，请参阅[打造个性化的用户体验](personalize-user-experience.md)。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="bd10e-106">For more details on personalization, see [Personalize the user experience](personalize-user-experience.md).</span></span>
