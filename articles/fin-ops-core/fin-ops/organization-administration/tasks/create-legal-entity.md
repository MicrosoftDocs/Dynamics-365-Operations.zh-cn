7. <span data-ttu-id="30b4e-118">展开 **地址** 部分。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="30b4e-118">Expand the **Addresses** section.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="30b4e-119">在 **地址** 部分，单击 **编辑** 输入街道名称和门牌号、邮政编码和城市之类的地址信息。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="30b4e-119">In the **Addresses** section, click **Edit** to enter address information, such as the street name and number, postal code, and city.</span></span>

12. <span data-ttu-id="30b4e-128">展开 **外贸和物流** 部分。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="30b4e-128">Expand the **Foreign trade and logistics** section.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="30b4e-129">在 **外贸和物流** 部分，输入法人的装运信息。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="30b4e-129">In the **Foreign trade and logistics** section, enter shipping information for the legal entity.</span></span>  
13. <span data-ttu-id="30b4e-130">展开 **编号规则** 部分。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="30b4e-130">Expand the **Number sequences** section.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="30b4e-131">在 **编号规则** 部分，您可以查看与法人关联的编号规则。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="30b4e-131">In the **Number sequences** section, you can view the number sequences that are associated with the legal entity.</span></span>
