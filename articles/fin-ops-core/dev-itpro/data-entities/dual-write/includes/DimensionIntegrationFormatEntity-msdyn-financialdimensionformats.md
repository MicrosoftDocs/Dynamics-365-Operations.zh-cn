## <a name="financial-dimension-format-to-msdyn_financialdimensionformats"></a>财务维度格式到 msdyn_financialdimensionformats

此模板在 Finance and Operations 应用与 Common Data Service 之间同步数据。

Finance and Operations 字段 | 映射类型 | 其他 Dynamics 365 字段 | 默认值
---|---|---|---
DIMENSIONFORMATNAME | = | msdyn_dimensionformatname | 
DIMENSIONFORMATTYPE | >< | msdyn_dimensionformattype | 
FINANCIALDIMENSIONFORMAT | = | msdyn_financialdimensionformat | 
ISACTIVE | >< | msdyn_isactive |
