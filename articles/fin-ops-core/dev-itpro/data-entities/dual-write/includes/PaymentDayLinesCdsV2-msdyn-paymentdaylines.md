## <a name="payment-day-lines-cds-v2-to-msdyn_paymentdaylines"></a>付款日行 CDS V2 到 msdyn_paymentdaylines

此模板在 Finance and Operations 应用与 Common Data Service 之间同步数据。

Finance and Operations 字段 | 映射类型 | 其他 Dynamics 365 字段 | 默认值
---|---|---|---
NAME | = | msdyn_paymentday.msdyn_name | 
LINENUMBER | = | msdyn_linenumber | 
FREQUENCY | >< | msdyn_frequency | 
DAYOFWEEK | >< | msdyn_dayofweek | 
DAYOFMONTH | = | msdyn_dayofmonth |
