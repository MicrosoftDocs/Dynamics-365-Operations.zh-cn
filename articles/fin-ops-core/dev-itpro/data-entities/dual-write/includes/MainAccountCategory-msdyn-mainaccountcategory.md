## <a name="main-account-categories-to-msdyn_mainaccountcategories"></a>主科目类别到 msdyn_mainaccountcategories

此模板在 Finance and Operations 应用与 Common Data Service 之间同步数据。

Finance and Operations 字段 | 映射类型 | 其他 Dynamics 365 字段 | 默认值
---|---|---|---
MAINACCOUNTCATEGORY | = | msdyn_mainaccountcategory | 
REFERENCEID | = | msdyn_referenceid | 
DESCRIPTION | = | msdyn_description | 
DISPLAYORDER | = | msdyn_displayorder | 
CLOSED | >< | msdyn_closed | 
MAINACCOUNTTYPE | >< | msdyn_mainaccounttypevalue |
