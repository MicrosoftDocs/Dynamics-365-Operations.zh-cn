## <a name="operating-unit-to-msdyn_internalorganizations"></a>运营单位到 msdyn_internalorganizations

此模板在 Finance and Operations 应用与 Common Data Service 之间同步数据。

Finance and Operations 字段 | 映射类型 | 其他 Dynamics 365 字段 | 默认值
---|---|---|---
LANGUAGEID | > | msdyn_languageid | 
NAMEALIAS | > | msdyn_namealias | 
NAME | > | msdyn_name | 
PARTYNUMBER | > | msdyn_partynumber | 
OPERATINGUNITTYPE | >> | msdyn_type |
