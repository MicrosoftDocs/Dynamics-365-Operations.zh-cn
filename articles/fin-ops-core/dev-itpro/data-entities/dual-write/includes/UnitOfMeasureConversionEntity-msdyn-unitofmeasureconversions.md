## <a name="unit-conversions-to-msdyn_unitofmeasureconversions"></a>单位转换到 msdyn_unitofmeasureconversions

此模板在 Finance and Operations 应用与 Common Data Service 之间同步数据。

Finance and Operations 字段 | 映射类型 | 其他 Dynamics 365 字段 | 默认值
---|---|---|---
DENOMINATOR | = | msdyn_denominator | 
NUMERATOR | = | msdyn_numerator | 
FACTOR | = | msdyn_factor | 
INNEROFFSET | = | msdyn_inneroffset | 
OUTEROFFSET | = | msdyn_outeroffset | 
ROUNDING | >< | msdyn_rounding | 
TOUNITSYMBOL | = | msdyn_tounit.msdyn_symbol | 
FROMUNITSYMBOL | = | msdyn_fromunit.msdyn_symbol |
