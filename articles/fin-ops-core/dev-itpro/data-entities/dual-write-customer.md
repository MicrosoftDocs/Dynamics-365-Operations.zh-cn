<span data-ttu-id="e3342-104">客户记录通常主控在多个应用程序中。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e3342-104">It's typical for customer records to be mastered in more than one application.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="e3342-105">例如，销售活动可通过 Sales 应用程序获取商业客户记录，而电子商务和零售则可通过 Finance and Operations 应用程序获取客户记录。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e3342-105">For example, sales activity can bring in commercial customer records through a Sales application, and e-Commerce or retail sales can bring in customer records through a Finance and Operations application.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="e3342-106">无论客户记录源自何处，都跨越应用程序边界和基础结构差异在后台集成。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e3342-106">Regardless of where the customer record originates, it's integrated behind the scenes across application boundaries and infrastructure differences.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="e3342-107">集成客户主控可以帮助处理多主控场景，并提供客户对 Dynamics 365 应用程序套件丰富的见解。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e3342-107">Integrated customer mastering helps handle multi-mastering scenarios and provides a comprehensive view of the customer to the Dynamics 365 application suite.</span></span>

## <a name="customer-data-flow"></a><span data-ttu-id="e3342-108">客户数据流</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e3342-108">Customer data flow</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="e3342-109">*客户*是应用程序中精心定义的概念。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e3342-109">*Customer* is a well-defined concept in applications.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="e3342-110">因此，客户数据的集成包括在这两个应用程序之间协调客户概念。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e3342-110">Therefore, the integration of customer data just involves harmonizing the customer concept between the two applications.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="e3342-111">下图显示客户数据流。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e3342-111">The following illustration shows the customer data flow.</span></span>

![客户数据流](media/dual-write-customer-data-flow.png)

<span data-ttu-id="e3342-113">客户可以大致分为两类：商业/组织客户和消费者/最终用户。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e3342-113">Customers can be broadly classified into two types: commercial/organizational customers and consumers/end users.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="e3342-114">这两种类型的客户存储在 Finance and Operations 和 Common Data Service 中并以不同方式处理。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e3342-114">These two types of customers are stored and handled differently in Finance and Operations and Common Data Service.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="e3342-115">在 Finance and Operations 中，商业/组织客户和消费者/最终用户在名为 **CustTable** (CustomerCustomerV3Entity) 的一个表中主控，并根据**类型**属性分类。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e3342-115">In Finance and Operations, both commercial/organizational customers and consumers/end users are mastered in a single table that is named **CustTable** (CustomerCustomerV3Entity), and they are classified based on the **Type** attribute.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="e3342-116">（如果**类型**设置为**组织**，则客户为商业/组织客户，如果**类型**设置为**个人**，则客户为消费者/最终用户。）主联系人信息通过 SMMContactPersonEntity 实体处理。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e3342-116">(If **Type** is set to **Organization**, the customer is a commercial/organizational customer, and if **Type** is set to **Person**, the customer is a consumer/end user.) The primary contact person information is handled through the SMMContactPersonEntity entity.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="e3342-117">在 Common Data Service 中，商业/组织客户在“客户”实体中主控，并在 **RelationshipType** 属性设置为**客户**时标识为客户。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e3342-117">In Common Data Service, commercial/organizational customers are mastered in the Account entity and are identified as customers when the **RelationshipType** attribute is set to **Customer**.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="e3342-118">消费者/最终用户和联系人均通过联系人实体表示。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e3342-118">Both consumers/end users and the contact person are represented by the Contact entity.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="e3342-119">为了提供客户/最终用户与联系人之间的清晰界限，**联系人**实体有一个布尔值标记，名称为 **Sellable**。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e3342-119">To provide a clear separation between a consumer/end user and a contact person, the **Contact** entity has a Boolean flag that is named **Sellable**.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="e3342-120">如果 **Sellable** 为 **True**，则联系人为消费者/最终用户，可以为该联系人创建报价单和订单。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e3342-120">When **Sellable** is **True**, the contact is a consumer/end user, and quotations and orders can be created for that contact.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="e3342-121">如果 **Sellable** 为 **False**，则联系人只是客户的主要联系人。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e3342-121">When **Sellable** is **False**, the contact is just a primary contact person of a customer.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="e3342-122">如果非适销联系人参与报价单或订单流程，则 **Sellable** 设置为 **True** 以将该联系人标记为适销联系人。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e3342-122">When a non-sellable contact participates in a quotation or order process, **Sellable** is set to **True** to flag the contact as a sellable contact.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="e3342-123">已成为适销联系人的联系人仍是适销联系人。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e3342-123">A contact that has become a sellable contact remains a sellable contact.</span></span>
