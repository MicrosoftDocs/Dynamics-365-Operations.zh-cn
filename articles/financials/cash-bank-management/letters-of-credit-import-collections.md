<a name="letters-of-credit"></a><span data-ttu-id="98dd8-107">信用证</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="98dd8-107">Letters of credit</span></span>
-----------------

<span data-ttu-id="98dd8-108">信用证用于国际交易记录并帮助确保将进行付款。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="98dd8-108">Letters of credit are used for international transactions and help guarantee that payments will be made.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="98dd8-109">信用证是银行签发的协议，在信用证中银行同意代表买家确认付款（如果满足了买家和卖家间的协议条款）。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="98dd8-109">A letter of credit is an agreement that is issued by a bank, in which the bank agrees to guarantee payment on behalf of a buyer, provided that the terms of the agreement between the buyer and seller are met.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="98dd8-110">信用证也指跟单信用卡 (DC)。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="98dd8-110">A letter of credit is also referred to as a documentary credit (DC).</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="98dd8-111">对于导入信用证，法人为信用证的买方或申请人。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="98dd8-111">For an import letter of credit, the legal entity is the buyer or the applicant for the letter of credit.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="98dd8-112">对于导出信用证，法人为信用证的卖方或受益人。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="98dd8-112">For an export letter of credit, the legal entity is the seller or the beneficiary of the letter of credit.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="98dd8-113">信用证涉及以下各方：</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="98dd8-113">The following parties are involved in a letter of credit:</span></span>

-   <span data-ttu-id="98dd8-114">打算支付货物的申请人（买方）</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="98dd8-114">The applicant (buyer) who intends to pay for the goods</span></span>
-   <span data-ttu-id="98dd8-115">将接收付款的受益人（卖方）</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="98dd8-115">The beneficiary (seller) who will receive the payment</span></span>
-   <span data-ttu-id="98dd8-116">签发信用证的签发银行</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="98dd8-116">The issuing bank that issues the letter of credit</span></span>
-   <span data-ttu-id="98dd8-117">代表申请人执行交易记录的通知银行</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="98dd8-117">The advising bank that carries out the transaction on behalf of the applicant</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="98dd8-118">信用证包括货物的描述、任何所需的凭证、装运数据以及未进行付款之前的到期日期。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="98dd8-118">The letter of credit includes a description of the goods, any required documents, the date of shipment, and the expiration date after which payment won't be made.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="98dd8-119">签发银行收取信用证的毛利。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="98dd8-119">The issuing bank collects a margin for the letter of credit.</span></span> 

<span data-ttu-id="98dd8-120">信用证可以是**可撤销**的或**不可撤销**的。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="98dd8-120">A letter of credit can be **revocable** or **irrevocable**.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="98dd8-121">信用证的性质可以是**可转让**、**不可转让**或**循环**。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="98dd8-121">The nature of a letter of credit can be **transferable**, **non-transferable**, or **revolving**.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="98dd8-122">通常，信用证是不可撤销的已确认的协议，将向提交完整且正确的装运文档的特定受益人付款。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="98dd8-122">Typically, a letter of credit is an irrevocable and confirmed agreement that payment will be made to a specific beneficiary upon submission of complete and accurate shipping documentation.</span></span>

## <a name="import-collections"></a><span data-ttu-id="98dd8-123">进口托收</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="98dd8-123">Import collections</span></span>
<span data-ttu-id="98dd8-124">进口托收是银行和出口商（卖方）直接的协议，在其中银行同意将装运文档传送到国际进口商（买方）。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="98dd8-124">An import collection is an agreement between the bank and the exporter (seller), in which the bank agrees to deliver the shipping documentation to the international importer (buyer).</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="98dd8-125">银行预期根据以现金显示的已装运货物的付款收据或根据针对付款的签名收据传送装运文档。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="98dd8-125">The bank is expected to deliver the shipping documentation upon receipt of payment for the shipped goods in cash, or upon receipt of a signed draft toward the payment.</span></span> 

<span data-ttu-id="98dd8-126">进口托收帮助确保卖家付款（当买家收集装运文档以获取进口货物交货时）。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="98dd8-126">An import collection helps guarantee that the seller is paid when the buyer collects the shipping documents to take delivery of the imported goods.</span></span>
