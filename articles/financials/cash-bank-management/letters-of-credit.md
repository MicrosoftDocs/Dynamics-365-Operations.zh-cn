# <a name="letters-of-credit"></a><span data-ttu-id="01dc9-103">信用证</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="01dc9-103">Letters of credit</span></span>

[!include[banner](../includes/banner.md)]


<span data-ttu-id="01dc9-104">信用证是通常用于跨国界限的货物采购和销售的银行凭证。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="01dc9-104">Letters of credit are bank documents that are commonly used for the purchase and sale of goods across international borders.</span></span> 

<span data-ttu-id="01dc9-105">信用证用于国际交易记录以确保将进行付款。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="01dc9-105">Letters of credit are used for international transactions to ensure that payments will be made.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="01dc9-106">信用证是银行签发的协议，在信用证中银行同意代表买家确认付款（如果满足了买家和卖家间的协议条款）。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="01dc9-106">A letter of credit is an agreement that is issued by a bank, in which the bank agrees to ensure payment on behalf of the buyer, if the terms of the agreement between the buyer and seller are met.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="01dc9-107">请注意，信用证也指跟单信用卡 (DC)。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="01dc9-107">Note that a letter of credit is also referred to as a documentary credit (DC).</span></span> 

<span data-ttu-id="01dc9-108">对于导入信用证，法人为信用证的买方或申请人。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="01dc9-108">For an import letter of credit, the legal entity is the buyer or the applicant for the letter of credit.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="01dc9-109">对于导出信用证，法人为信用证的卖方或受益人。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="01dc9-109">For an export letter of credit, the legal entity is the seller or the beneficiary of the letter of credit.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="01dc9-110">信用证涉及以下各方：</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="01dc9-110">The following parties are involved with a letter of credit:</span></span> 

 - <span data-ttu-id="01dc9-111">打算支付货物的申请人（买方）</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="01dc9-111">The applicant (buyer) who intends to pay for the goods</span></span> 
 - <span data-ttu-id="01dc9-112">将接收付款的受益人（卖方）</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="01dc9-112">The beneficiary (seller) who will receive the payment</span></span>
 - <span data-ttu-id="01dc9-113">签发信用证的签发银行</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="01dc9-113">The issuing bank that issues the letter of credit</span></span>
 - <span data-ttu-id="01dc9-114">代表申请人执行交易记录的通知银行</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="01dc9-114">The advising bank that carries out the transaction on behalf of the applicant</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="01dc9-115">信用证包括货物的描述、任何所需的凭证、装运数据以及未进行付款之前的到期日期。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="01dc9-115">The letter of credit includes a description of the goods, any required documents, the date of shipment, and the expiration date after which payment will not be made.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="01dc9-116">签发银行收取信用证的毛利。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="01dc9-116">The issuing bank collects a margin for the letter of credit.</span></span> 

<span data-ttu-id="01dc9-117">信用证可以是可撤销的或不可撤销的。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="01dc9-117">A letter of credit can be revocable or irrevocable.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="01dc9-118">信用证的性质可以是或可转让、不可转让或循环。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="01dc9-118">The nature of a letter of credit can be transferable, non transferable, or revolving.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="01dc9-119">通常，信用证是不可撤销的已确认的协议，将向提交完整且正确的装运文档的特定受益人付款。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="01dc9-119">Typically, a letter of credit is an irrevocable and confirmed agreement that payment will be made to a specific beneficiary upon submission of complete and accurate shipping documentation.</span></span>
