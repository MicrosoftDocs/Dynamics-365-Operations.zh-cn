# <a name="tender-based-discounts"></a><span data-ttu-id="8d8f8-103">基于支付方式的折扣</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="8d8f8-103">Tender-based discounts</span></span>

[!include [banner](includes/banner.md)]


<span data-ttu-id="8d8f8-104">发布自有的品牌信用卡是零售商的常见做法。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="8d8f8-104">It's a common practice among retailers to release private, branded credit cards.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="8d8f8-105">零售商将从中受益，因为他们会从银行获得优惠价格。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="8d8f8-105">The retailers benefit because they get preferred rates from the banks.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="8d8f8-106">此外，由于这些信用卡可以鼓励客户更多地前往商店，因此有助于提高零售商的利润。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="8d8f8-106">Additionally, because these credits cards can encourage customers to visit the store more often, they help improve the retailer's bottom line.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="8d8f8-107">因此，零售商具有提高顾客对其品牌信用卡的使用量的动机。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="8d8f8-107">Therefore, retailers have an incentive to increase customer use of their branded credit cards.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="8d8f8-108">为了实现此目标，他们通常向使用这些信用卡的客户提供额外的折扣。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="8d8f8-108">To achieve this goal, they often provide additional discounts to customers who use these credit cards.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="8d8f8-109">或者，不提供品牌信用卡的零售商可能希望鼓励客户使用其他支付方式付款，如现金、礼品卡或会员积分。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="8d8f8-109">Alternatively, retailers who don't provide branded credit cards might want to encourage customers to pay by using other tender types, such as cash, gift cards, or loyalty points.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="8d8f8-110">这样，可以帮助减少信用卡处理费费用。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="8d8f8-110">In this way, they can help reduce the expense of credit card processing fees.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="8d8f8-111">因此，零售商可能为使用这些替代支付方式的客户提供折扣。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="8d8f8-111">Therefore, retailers might provide discounts to customers who use these alternative tender types.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="8d8f8-112">在 Microsoft Dynamics 365 Commerce 中，零售商可以配置折扣率，如果客户使用首选支付方式付款，则折扣率将应用于符合条件的行。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="8d8f8-112">In Microsoft Dynamics 365 Commerce, retailers can configure a discount percentage that is applied to qualified lines if the customer pays by using the preferred tender type.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="8d8f8-113">客户可以决定是进行部分付款还是全额付款，然后 Commerce 会确定适当的折扣金额。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="8d8f8-113">The customer can decide whether to do a partial payment or a full payment, and Commerce determines the appropriate discount amount.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="8d8f8-114">请注意，折扣始终是针对符合条件的商品的税前金额提供。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="8d8f8-114">Note that the discount is always given on the pre-tax amount of the qualified items.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="8d8f8-115">基于支付方式的折扣不会与基于商品的折扣（如定期或手动折扣）冲突。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="8d8f8-115">Tender-based discounts don't compete with item-based discounts, such as periodic or manual discounts.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="8d8f8-116">它们始终与商品折扣合并在一起。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="8d8f8-116">They are always compounded over the item discounts.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="8d8f8-117">因此，即使对商品应用了专有的定期折扣，基于支付方式的折扣仍会在专有定期折扣之前应用。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="8d8f8-117">Therefore, even if an exclusive periodic discount is applied to an item, the tender-based discount is still applied on top of the exclusive periodic discount.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="8d8f8-118">同样，如果将阈值折扣应用于交易，并且基于支付方式的折扣将总计降低到阈值以下，则阈值折扣仍将应用于交易。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="8d8f8-118">Likewise, if a threshold discount is applied to the transaction, and the tender-based discount reduces the total below the threshold, the threshold discount is still applied to the transaction.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="8d8f8-119">即使基于支付方式的折扣减少了交易的小计，应用于交易的自动费用也不会受到影响。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="8d8f8-119">Even though tender-based discounts reduce the subtotal of the transaction, automatic charges that are applied to the transaction aren't affected.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="8d8f8-120">例如，如果由于小计超过 100 美元而将配送费用计算为 5 美元，而基于支付方式的折扣减少了金额，使其低于 100 美元，该订单的配送费用则仍为 5 美元。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="8d8f8-120">For example, if the delivery charges are calculated as $5 because the subtotal was more than $100, and the tender-based discount reduces the amount so that it's less than $100, the delivery charges are still $5 for the order.</span></span>


> [!NOTE]
> <span data-ttu-id="8d8f8-121">基于支付方式的折扣将按比例分配给符合条件的销售订单行，减少各个行的税前金额。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="8d8f8-121">Tender-based discounts are proportionally distributed to the qualified sales lines and reduce the pre-tax amount of the individual lines.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="8d8f8-122">如果为支付方式（例如，现金）配置了多个基于支付方式的折扣，则仅应用最佳的基于支付方式的折扣。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="8d8f8-122">If multiple tender-based discounts are configured for a tender type (for example, cash), only the best tender-based discount is applied.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="8d8f8-123">基于支付方式的折扣只能应用于未锁定价格的销售订单行。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="8d8f8-123">Tender-based discounts can be applied only to sales lines where the prices aren't locked.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="8d8f8-124">如果将新的销售订单行添加到订单，则基于支付方式的折扣仅在付款期间才会应用于新销售订单行。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="8d8f8-124">If new sales lines are added to an order, the tender-based discount is applied to the new sales lines only during payment.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="8d8f8-125">在下达提货或装运的客户订单时，基于支付方式的折扣仅应用于保证金金额。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="8d8f8-125">While a customer order for pickup or shipment is being placed, the tender-based discount is applied only to the deposit amount.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="8d8f8-126">下达订单后，在履行期间，销售订单行的价格将被锁定。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="8d8f8-126">After the order is placed, during fulfillment, the prices of the sales lines are locked.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="8d8f8-127">因此，对于在提货过程中支付或在装运过程中授权的任何余额，不会应用基于支付方式的折扣。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="8d8f8-127">Therefore, no tender-based discount is applied to any balance that is paid during pickup or authorized during shipment.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="8d8f8-128">仅当零售商在下达订单时收集全部金额作为定金时，才可以将基于支付方式的折扣应用于客户订单的全部金额。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="8d8f8-128">The tender-based discount can be applied to the whole amount of a customer order only if the retailer collects the whole amount as a deposit while the order is being placed.</span></span>

> [!IMPORTANT]
> <span data-ttu-id="8d8f8-129">在 Commerce 中，基于支付方式的折扣目前仅限于两种付款类型：信用卡和现金。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="8d8f8-129">In Commerce, tender-based discounts are currently limited to two payment types: credit cards and cash.</span></span>
