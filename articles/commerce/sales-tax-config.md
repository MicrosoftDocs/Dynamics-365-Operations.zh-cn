# <a name="configure-sales-tax-for-online-orders"></a><span data-ttu-id="2c910-103">为在线订单配置销售税</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2c910-103">Configure sales tax for online orders</span></span>

[!include [banner](includes/banner.md)]

<span data-ttu-id="2c910-104">本主题提供使用基于目的地或基于客户帐户的税务设置为不同的在线订单类型选择销售税组的概述。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2c910-104">This topic provides an overview of sales tax group selection for different online order types using either destination-based or customer account-based tax settings.</span></span> 

<span data-ttu-id="2c910-105">您可能希望您的电子商务渠道支持在线订单交货或提货等选项。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2c910-105">You may want your e-commerce channel to support options like delivery or pickup for online orders.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="2c910-106">销售税适用性基于您的在线客户选择的选项。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2c910-106">The sales tax applicability is based on the option selected by your online customers.</span></span> 

## <a name="destination-based-taxes-for-online-orders"></a><span data-ttu-id="2c910-107">在线订单的基于目的地的税务</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2c910-107">Destination-based taxes for online orders</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="2c910-108">通常，装运到客户地址的在线订单的税金由目的地定义。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2c910-108">In general, taxes for online orders that ship to customer addresses are defined by the destination.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="2c910-109">每个销售税组都有一个基于零售目的地的税务配置，在此配置中，您的企业可以通过分层形式定义目的地详细信息，例如国家/地区、省/自治区/直辖市、县和城市。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2c910-109">Every sales tax group has a retail destination-based tax configuration in which your business can define destination details, such as country or region, state, county, and city in a hierarchical form.</span></span>

### <a name="orders-delivered-to-customer-address"></a><span data-ttu-id="2c910-110">交货到客户地址的订单</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2c910-110">Orders delivered to customer address</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="2c910-111">下达在线订单后，Commerce 税务引擎使用订单中每个明细项的交货地址，按照匹配的基于目的地的税务条件查找销售税组。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2c910-111">When an online order is placed, the Commerce tax engine uses the delivery address of every line item in the order and finds sales tax groups with matching destination-based tax criteria.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="2c910-112">例如，对于行项交货地址是到加利福尼亚州旧金山的在线订单，税引擎将查找加利福尼亚的销售税组和销售税代码，然后相应地计算每个行项的税金。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2c910-112">For example, for an online order with a line item delivery address to San Francisco, California, the tax engine will find the sales tax group and sales tax code for California and then calculate tax for each line item accordingly.</span></span>

### <a name="order-pick-up-in-store"></a><span data-ttu-id="2c910-113">订单在店内提货</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2c910-113">Order pick up in store</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="2c910-114">对于指定了店内提货或懒人提货的订单行，将应用所选提货商店的税组。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2c910-114">For order lines with pick up in store or curbside pickup specified, the tax group from the selected pickup store will be applied.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="2c910-115">有关如何为给定商店设置销售税的详细信息，请参阅[为商店设置其他税务选项](/dynamicsax-2012/appuser-itpro/set-other-tax-options-for-stores)。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2c910-115">For details about how to set up sales taxes for a given store, see [Set other tax options for stores](/dynamicsax-2012/appuser-itpro/set-other-tax-options-for-stores).</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="2c910-120">可供您为客户帐户配置销售税的两个位置包括：</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2c910-120">The two places where you configure sales tax for a customer account are:</span></span>
