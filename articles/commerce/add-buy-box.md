<span data-ttu-id="bdcf5-106">术语*购买框*通常指的是产品详细信息页中的“第一屏”区域，用于承载进行产品购买所需全部最重要信息。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="bdcf5-106">The term *buy box* typically refers to the area of a product details page that is "above the fold," and that hosts all the most important information that is required to make a product purchase.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="bdcf5-107">（“第一屏”区域在首次加载页面时显示，所以用户不必向下滚动即可看到。）</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="bdcf5-107">(An area that is "above the fold" is visible when the page is first loaded, so that users don't have to scroll down to see it.)</span></span>
